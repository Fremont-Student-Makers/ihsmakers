Purpose

We are partnered with Fremont Student Makers to develop a CubeSat program that will launch Fremont's first successful student satellite into orbit.

Description

Students will design, build, and test a fully operational 1U CubeSat capable of gathering scientific data from space. Our program aims to give high school students a chance to participate in a real-world application of their engineering skills while collaborating with aerospace partners for launch opportunities.

Through this project, we provide an environment that encourages growth of skills vital for future career settings, including systems design, electrical engineering, software development, and project management.

Our Objectives

  • Design a 1U CubeSat frame and payload
  • Develop onboard systems for data collection and transmission
  • Collaborate with aerospace partners for launch opportunities
  • Gather data on atmospheric conditions and solar radiation
  • Document and share findings with the scientific community
