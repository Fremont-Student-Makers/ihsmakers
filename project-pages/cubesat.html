CubeSat Program

About the Project

Our CubeSat program is dedicated to launching Irvington High's first successful satellite into orbit.

Students will design, build, and test a fully operational CubeSat capable of gathering scientific data from space.

Our program aims to give highschool students a chance to participate in a project that allows them to partake in a real-world application of their engineering skills.

Through this project, we hope to provide an environment that encourages growth of skills vital for future career settings.

Objectives

  • Design a 1U CubeSat frame and payload.
  • Collaborate with aerospace partners for launch opportunities.
  • Gather data on atmospheric conditions and solar radiation.
