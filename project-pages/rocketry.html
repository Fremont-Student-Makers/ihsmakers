Purpose

The IHS Rocketry Team is dedicated to fostering a passion for aerospace innovation through hands-on experience in rocket design, construction, and launch operations.

Description

Our rocketry team designs, builds, and launches model rockets as part of national competitions such as the American Rocketry Challenge (TARC). Members learn practical skills in physics, systems engineering, safety protocols, and teamwork while pursuing a passion for aerospace.

With FAA-certified mentors and professional-grade equipment, we provide students with authentic aerospace engineering experience, from theoretical design to real-world launch operations.

Activities & Events

  • Rocket design and simulations using OpenRocket software
  • Weekly build sessions and team meetings
  • Launch days with FAA-certified mentors
  • Participation in national challenges and STEM competitions
  • Safety training and compliance certifications
