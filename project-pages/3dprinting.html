3D Printing Lab

Purpose

Our 3D Printing Lab empowers students to bring their ideas to life, producing functional prototypes, custom parts, and artistic designs for various club projects.

Description

The 3D Printing Lab serves as the manufacturing heartbeat of our club's builds, enabling rapid prototyping and custom component creation. From rocketry nosecones to CubeSat components, students learn cutting-edge additive manufacturing techniques.
